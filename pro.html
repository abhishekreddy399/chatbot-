Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about OrderTrack AI.

How does OrderTrack AI work?
OrderTrack AI connects to your email and shopping accounts to automatically detect orders. Our AI then tracks each package across all carriers, provides real-time updates, and can answer any questions you have about your deliveries through natural conversation.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and never store your payment information. Your data is only used to provide order tracking services and is never shared with third parties. You can disconnect accounts at any time.
Which retailers and carriers do you support?
We support all major retailers (Amazon, Walmart, Target, etc.) and carriers (UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL). Our system is constantly updated to add new stores and shipping services. If we don't support a retailer yet, you can manually add tracking numbers.
Can I track orders from multiple email addresses?
Yes! You can connect multiple email accounts to your OrderTrack AI profile. The system will automatically scan all connected accounts for order confirmations and tracking information, consolidating everything in one dashboard.
How accurate are the delivery predictions?
Our AI analyzes millions of data points including carrier performance, weather conditions, and historical delivery times to predict your delivery window. Currently, our predictions are accurate within 2 hours for 92% of deliveries.
